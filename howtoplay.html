HOW TO PLAY?

This game is played by four players one of them is a bomber (doesnt know about the subject),

There are two catogeries animals and food,

Each player press his number button to show his role and after that he click again to hide it,

You have 2 minutes to questions each other about the subject,

After the two minutes every player will vote on the player he suspect,

After voting press the button reveal to show the real bomber,

Then the bomber should guess the right subject.
